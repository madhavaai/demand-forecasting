ROBUSTNESS VERDICT (current-day price):
Dropping current-day price raises WAPE by +0.0332 (0.4170 vs 0.3838) — the model
relies on it substantially. This is the honest weak point: current-day
average price is treated as known-in-advance (the retailer sets it),
but if that assumption fails, price_lag_1 must be used instead.
CRUCIALLY: even with current-day price removed entirely, WAPE
0.4170 still beats the seasonal-naive baseline (0.5859) by 29% —
so the model's value survives the worst-case assumption.
